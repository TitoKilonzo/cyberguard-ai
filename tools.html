Tips

How to Use These Tools Effectively

Be Specific
Include as many details as possible — the more context you provide, the more accurate and useful the AI analysis will be. Vague descriptions lead to vague results.
Copy Full Messages
For spam analysis, paste the complete email or SMS including sender address, subject line, and full body. Partial text may miss critical indicators.
Second Opinion Only
AI analysis is a powerful guide but not infallible. Use it alongside your own judgment and, for high-stakes decisions, consult a security professional.
