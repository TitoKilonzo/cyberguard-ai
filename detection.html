Three Pillars of AI Detection

Anomaly Detection
AI establishes a behavioral baseline and flags statistically significant deviations — catching insider threats, compromised accounts, and lateral movement attempts.
Behavioral AI
Signature + Heuristics
Combines traditional signature matching with heuristic analysis to catch both known malware families and previously unseen variants with high accuracy.
Hybrid Model
Network Traffic Analysis
Deep packet inspection powered by AI detects C2 communication, data exfiltration attempts, and encrypted malicious traffic in real time.
Deep Inspection
Impact

Real-Time Alerts & Response

The impact of AI threat detection extends beyond identification — AI coordinates the full response, reducing mean time to respond (MTTR) from hours to seconds.

Proactive Threat Detection
AI identifies threats before they escalate, providing security teams with early warning intelligence and actionable context for faster decisions.
Prioritized Alerts
AI scores and prioritizes incidents by severity and business impact, ensuring the most critical threats are addressed first by human teams.
Automated Containment
When a threat is confirmed, AI automatically isolates affected systems, blocks malicious IPs, and revokes compromised credentials immediately.
